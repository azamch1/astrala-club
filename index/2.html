A website that is committed to improving society through helping the children of the world.

Five Year Plan

1) Astrala Clinic

(The clinic will be a nonprofit clinic that will be primarily designed to assist the children and the senior citizens)

2) Astrala Public School

    (A primary private public school for the children.  In Pakistan, especially in the rural areas the literacy rate is very low.  The majority is unable to get education only because of the poverty factor.  As the parents can not bear the expenses of their children they force them to work at various places such as in shops, industry, etc.  We can contribute by opening a school for the children where we can provide free education.  The goal is to give the children the tools to grow and become socially and economically dependent.)

3) Astrala Community

(A home or place for the homeless, old aged, orphans, and any one     whom is need)

4) Sponsoring Students

    (We can sponsor students at various schools by covering their expenses such as tuition, fees, uniforms, books, etc.)

The five-year plan consists of four major steps that are currently being implemented.  The clinic is the first step and has opened in Pakistan but it is operating off of a very limited budget.  The rest of the projects will be implemented over the next five years with time being dependent on the financial situation.  That is why it is so important for private contributions to help this project.  Without your contribution we will not be able to expand the lifeline and help those who need it the most.
